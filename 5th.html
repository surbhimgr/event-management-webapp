Cultural Fest

A cultural fest is a fest that showcases the culture adopted by the college organizing it. Every year, a college adopts a particular theme and organizes various events related to it. The college premises are also usually decorated accordingly, thus giving it all a “cultural” look. A cultural fest is the flagship event of the college and is open to students from all colleges irrespective of their fields/specializations, thus providing a great opportunity to mingle and network with others from various walks of life.

About  Cultural  Fest

The annual cultural fest is devoted to create an indelible experience for all the vibrant spectators, simultaneously providing unique opportunities to youth for showcasing their talents in front of extremely large audiences and top-notch impartial judges. Cultural fest started as intra college event and today it hosts well known inter college events.
